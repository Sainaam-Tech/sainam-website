## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

<details class="mb-4">
<summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-lg">
What is MVP development?
</summary>

MVP development is the process of building a minimum viable product with only the core features needed to validate a business idea. The goal is to launch quickly, gather real user feedback, and improve the product based on actual usage.
</details>

<details class="mb-4">
<summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-lg">
How long does MVP development usually take?
</summary>

MVP development typically takes **6 to 12 weeks**, depending on complexity, scope, and integrations. Simple MVPs can be built faster, while more complex products may require additional time.
</details>

<details class="mb-4">
<summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-lg">
How much does MVP development cost?
</summary>

The cost of MVP development depends on scope, features, and technical requirements. Once the scope is finalized, we provide a clear estimate focused on building a cost-effective MVP without unnecessary features.
</details>

<details class="mb-4">
<summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-lg">
Is MVP development suitable for non-technical founders?
</summary>

Yes. Many founders we work with are non-technical. We handle technical decisions, explain trade-offs clearly, and guide you through every stage of the MVP development process.
</details>

<details class="mb-4">
<summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-lg">
Can an MVP be scaled into a full product later?
</summary>

Yes. While MVPs are lean by design, we build them with scalability in mind so they can grow into full products as your user base and requirements expand.
</details>
